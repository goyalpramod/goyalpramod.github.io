Learn to Learn

Learning is something we do everyday, but have you ever tried learning how to learn?

That seems like a strange question to ask, until you start pondering over it. Think of it in terms of an optimization problem, we try to optimize our lifts in the gym, we try to optimize the way we spend our money, we try to optimize our time. Logically; Why can‚Äôt we optimize our learning.

‚ÄúThrow everything at the wall, see what sticks‚Äù

This is a great saying in my opinion, As we keep learning (real learning, not just memorizing and giving tests) we find many ways in the world to do so, Audiobooks, video tutorials, Blogs, Hands-on learning and so many more(flashcards much!!). SMART; Specific, Measurable, Achievable, Relevant, and Time-bound

‚ÄúLose weight‚Äù

That is something all of us have set as a new resolution at some point of time in our lives. Let‚Äôs see how we can make it better using SMART.

Be smart, use SMART.

Enjoy; What‚Äôs the point if you are not having fun

One of my favorite things to do is imagine what it would be like after I am able to do what I am trying to learn at the moment.

The joy of that vision is greater than any difficulty faced during the learning process.

Keep an open mind, gather your tools, Gradually fight harder daemons, be smart about your method, enjoy your victory!!!
